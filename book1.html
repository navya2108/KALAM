WHISPERS OF THE DARK

Finding this book means that you’ve visited the Whispers of the Dark, where you can't understand what’s happening or you don't care enough to find out. This book will maybe put words into that strange feeling that you can’t comprehend or are scared of.

“Whispers of the dark” will take care of you, wrap you in a warm blanket and make you find peace in the pages of seamless thought.
